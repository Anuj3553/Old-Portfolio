Refund Policy for Portfolio Website

Thank you for choosing our portfolio website services. We strive to provide you with the best experience and utmost satisfaction. However, in the event that you are not completely satisfied with our services or encounter any issues, we have outlined our refund policy below to address your concerns.



1. Service Eligibility

Our refund policy applies to all services provided by our portfolio website development team, including design, development, and maintenance services.



2. Refund Request

If you are dissatisfied with our services, please contact our customer support team within 30 days of the project's completion. Refund requests must be submitted in writing, either through email or our designated support channels.



3. Valid Refund Reasons

We offer refunds under the following circumstances:

a. Failure to meet project requirements: If our team fails to deliver the agreed-upon project specifications outlined in the initial contract or project brief.

b. Technical issues: If there are technical problems or errors that prevent the website from functioning as intended and cannot be resolved within a reasonable time frame.

c. Service cancellation: If you decide to cancel the project before any work has begun or within the early stages of development.



4. Non-Refundable Situations

We do not offer refunds in the following situations:

a. Failure to meet project requirements: If our team fails to deliver the agreed-upon project specifications outlined in the initial contract or project brief.

c. Service cancellation: If you decide to cancel the project before any work has begun or within the early stages of development.



5. Refund Process

6. Communication and Resolution

We are committed to resolving any concerns or issues you may have. We encourage open communication and urge you to reach out to our customer support team with any questions or problems you encounter before requesting a refund. Our team will make every effort to address and resolve your concerns promptly.



7. Modifications to the Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify or amend this refund policy at our discretion. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the revised policy on our website. It is your responsibility to review the policy periodically.
